Our Process — How LeadBridge Works

A clear, repeatable process that balances scale and quality: we target, collect, verify, enrich and deliver leads that your reps can act on immediately.

Discovery & ICP

We collaborate to define geography, industry, company size and decision-maker profile so lists are aligned with your goals.

Multi-Source Collection

We pull signals from public records, networks, and platforms to create a broad candidate set for verification.

Human Validation

Phone and email checks are performed to remove invalid contacts and to identify decision makers and roles.

Enrichment & Tagging

Each record is enriched with company size, role and campaign tags to enable targeted outreach strategies.

Delivery Formats

CSV, Google Sheets or CRM-ready files — with campaign-ready columns and segmentation tags for fast execution.

Ongoing Support

Weekly refreshes, sample previews and hands-on support to continuously improve conversion and ROI.
